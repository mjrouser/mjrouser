Homeless Data Warehouse

Groundbreaking technology for unifying homeless client data across the continuum of care (CoC). Rails app digests data according to HUD standards and uses Machine learning to de-duplicate client records and provide data quality incites. Completely open source and now being deployed by other CoCs.
